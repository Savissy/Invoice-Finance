The Problem

Traditional invoice financing presents significant challenges for businesses worldwide, particularly small and medium enterprises (SMEs):

‚è∞

Cash Flow Constraints

Businesses often wait 30-90 days for invoice payments, creating severe cash flow bottlenecks that hinder operations and growth.

üè¶

Limited Access to Capital

Traditional banks and factoring companies impose strict credit requirements, lengthy approval processes, and high fees that exclude many businesses.

üí∏

High Costs

Factoring fees typically range from 1-5% of invoice value, with additional hidden charges significantly reducing business profitability.

üîí

Lack of Transparency

Opaque pricing structures, complex terms, and limited visibility into the funding process create uncertainty and mistrust.

Our Solution

Invoice Finance leverages blockchain technology to create a transparent, efficient, and accessible invoice financing marketplace that benefits both invoice issuers and investors.

üí° Key Innovation

By tokenizing invoices as NFTs on the Cardano blockchain, we create verifiable, tradeable digital assets that can be funded instantly by a global pool of investors, eliminating traditional intermediaries and reducing costs.

How It Works

Our platform operates on a simple three-step process:

1

Tokenize

Business uploads their invoice to the platform. The invoice is verified and automatically minted as a unique NFT on the Cardano blockchain with all relevant metadata.

2

Fund

Investors browse available invoice NFTs in the marketplace and fund invoices they find attractive. Smart contracts handle the secure transfer of funds to the invoice issuer.

3

Repay

When the original debtor pays the invoice, the issuer repays the investor through the smart contract. The investor receives the agreed-upon return, and the NFT is burned.

System Architecture

Invoice Finance is built on a robust, decentralized architecture leveraging Cardano blockchain technology for maximum security, transparency, and efficiency.

Technical Stack

  • Blockchain: Cardano (Proof-of-Stake)
  • Smart Contracts: Plutus (Cardano's native smart contract language)
  • Wallet Integration: Cardano wallet connectors (Nami, Eternl, Flint)
  • NFT Standard: CIP-25 (Cardano NFT metadata standard)
  • Frontend: Modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript)

System Flow Diagram

Data Flow

  1. Invoice Upload: Issuer uploads invoice document through the web interface
  2. Verification: Platform validates invoice authenticity and extracts metadata
  3. NFT Minting: Smart contract mints unique NFT representing the invoice
  4. Marketplace Listing: NFT appears in the available invoices marketplace
  5. Investor Funding: Investor reviews and funds the invoice NFT
  6. Fund Transfer: Smart contract transfers funds from investor to issuer
  7. Repayment: Issuer repays investor when original debtor pays
  8. NFT Burning: Upon successful repayment, NFT is burned to complete the cycle

If you're a business looking to unlock working capital by financing your invoices, follow this comprehensive guide.

Smart Contract Functions

1. Mint Invoice NFT

Creates a unique NFT representing the invoice with embedded metadata.

  • Validates invoice data
  • Generates unique asset identifier
  • Records issuer address as initial owner
  • Lists NFT in marketplace contract

2. Fund Invoice

Transfers funds from investor to issuer and updates NFT ownership.

  • Verifies investor has sufficient funds
  • Transfers face value minus discount to issuer
  • Transfers NFT ownership to investor
  • Updates invoice status to "funded"
  • Records transaction on-chain

3. Process Repayment

Handles repayment from issuer to investor upon invoice maturity.

  • Verifies issuer identity
  • Transfers repayment amount to investor
  • Burns the invoice NFT
  • Updates status to "repaid"
  • Records completion on-chain

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is invoice financing?

Invoice financing allows businesses to access immediate cash by selling their outstanding invoices at a discount. Instead of waiting 30-90 days for customer payment, businesses receive funds immediately and repay investors when the customer pays.

Why use blockchain for invoice financing?

Blockchain technology provides transparency, security, and efficiency. Smart contracts automate the entire process, eliminate intermediaries, reduce costs, and create immutable records of all transactions. NFTs make invoices tradeable digital assets.

Do I need cryptocurrency experience?

While basic understanding of cryptocurrency wallets is helpful, our platform is designed to be user-friendly. Follow our getting started guide to set up your wallet and begin using the platform within minutes.

What happens if an invoice isn't repaid?

While we implement verification measures, invoice financing carries inherent risk. We recommend investors diversify across multiple invoices and issuers. We're developing a reputation system and dispute resolution mechanism for future releases.

For Invoice Issuers

What types of invoices can I finance?

You can finance B2B invoices for goods or services already delivered to creditworthy customers. Invoices should be legitimate, unpaid, and not yet at the due date. We currently don't support invoices to individual consumers or government contracts.

How quickly can I get funded?

Once you create your invoice NFT, funding can happen within minutes if investors find your terms attractive. On average, competitive invoices are funded within 1-4 hours.

What discount rate should I offer?

Typical discount rates range from 3-8% depending on invoice amount, due date, and your reputation. Check similar invoices in the marketplace to gauge competitive rates. Higher discounts (lower costs to you) attract investors faster.

For Investors

What returns can I expect?

Returns typically range from 3-8% for short-term financing (30-90 days), which annualizes to 12-32%. Actual returns depend on the discount rate offered by issuers and the invoice term length.

How do I assess risk?

Review the invoice details, issuer's repayment history, and customer reputation. Start with smaller investments, diversify across multiple invoices, and consider invoice maturity dates. Higher returns generally indicate higher perceived risk.

Can I sell my invoice NFT before maturity?

Currently, invoice NFTs are held until repayment. We're developing a secondary marketplace where investors can sell their invoice NFTs to other investors before maturity, providing additional liquidity.

Technical Questions

Which Cardano wallets are supported?

We support all major Cardano wallets including Nami, Eternl, Flint, Yoroi, and any wallet implementing the CIP-30 standard (Cardano dApp connector).

What are the network fees?

Cardano network fees (transaction fees) are minimal, typically 0.17-0.35 ADA per transaction. These fees go to stake pool operators who secure the network, not to Invoice Finance.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Invoice metadata is stored on the Cardano blockchain using IPFS for document storage. All transactions are secured by cryptography. Your wallet private keys never leave your device.

Can I use this from any country?

Invoice Finance is accessible globally as a decentralized platform. However, users are responsible for complying with their local regulations regarding cryptocurrency use and business financing.
